Reaction Time Test

Select Test Mode:

Click to start

How to play:

Model 1: Reaction Time Only

  • Click the box to start
  • Wait for the color to change to green
  • Click as fast as you can when it turns green
  • Your reaction time will be displayed

Model 2: Reaction + Movement Time

  • Click the box to start
  • Wait for the color to change to green
  • Click as fast as you can when it turns green
  • A target will appear 200 pixels away from your click
  • Reach and click the target as fast as possible
  • Both reaction and movement times will be displayed
